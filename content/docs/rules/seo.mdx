# SEO Rules

SEO rules account for 7% of the overall score. They evaluate whether the site can be discovered by potential guests.

## Why SEO Is Weighted Lower

- Most STR traffic is direct or referral
- OTAs dominate search results
- Conversion matters more than discovery

## Rules

### sitemap_missing

**No sitemap.xml found**

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Severity | Major |
| Impact | 0.45 |
| Effort | Low |

A sitemap helps search engines discover and index all pages.

**Fix:** Generate and submit a sitemap to Google Search Console.

---

### schema_missing

**No LocalBusiness or LodgingBusiness schema**

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Severity | Minor |
| Impact | 0.30 |
| Effort | Low |

Structured data helps search engines understand what your site is about.

**Fix:** Add LodgingBusiness or VacationRental schema markup.

---

### noindex_money_pages

**Property or booking pages blocked from indexing**

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Severity | Blocker |
| Impact | 0.80 |
| Effort | Low |

If your most important pages have `noindex` tags, search engines won't show them.

**Fix:** Remove noindex from revenue-generating pages.

---

### missing_meta_descriptions

**Key pages missing meta descriptions**

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Severity | Minor |
| Impact | 0.25 |
| Effort | Low |

Meta descriptions appear in search results.

**Fix:** Add unique, compelling meta descriptions (150-160 characters).
