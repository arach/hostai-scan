# Rules by Category

[← View all in one table](/docs/all-rules)

## Category Weights

| Category | Weight | Focus |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Conversion | 32% | Booking flow friction |
| Performance | 18% | Load speed, responsiveness |
| Trust | 18% | Reviews, identity, credibility |
| Content | 14% | Photos, descriptions, messaging |
| Analytics | 8% | Tracking and measurement |
| SEO | 7% | Discoverability |
| Security | 3% | HTTPS, TLS |

## Conversion Rules (35%)

| Rule ID | Title | Sev | Impact | Fix |
|---------|-------|-----|--------|-----|
| `missing_primary_cta` | No booking CTA above fold (mobile) | B | 0.95 | Add prominent CTA in hero section |
| `no_sticky_cta_mobile` | No persistent CTA on scroll | M | 0.75 | Add sticky booking bar |
| `click_depth_high` | >3 clicks to book | M/B | 0.75-0.90 | Streamline navigation |
| `missing_cancellation` | Cancellation policy hidden | M | 0.70 | Show policy near CTA |
| `fees_not_visible_early` | Fees hidden until checkout | M | 0.65 | Show full price breakdown early |
| `requires_account` | Account required to book | M | 0.60 | Allow guest checkout |
| `no_instant_booking` | Inquiry only, no instant book | m | 0.40 | Enable instant booking |
| `cross_domain_booking` | Redirects to third-party | m | 0.35 | Embed booking on your domain |

**Sev:** B=Blocker, M=Major, m=Minor

## Performance Rules (20%)

| Rule ID | Title | Sev | Impact | Thresholds |
|---------|-------|-----|--------|------------|
| `mobile_lcp_slow` | Largest Contentful Paint slow | M/B | 0.70-0.90 | Good ≤2.5s, Poor >4s, Blocker >5s |
| `cls_poor` | Layout shifts | M/B | 0.55-0.75 | Good ≤0.1, Poor >0.25 |
| `inp_high` | Interaction lag | M/B | 0.60-0.80 | Good ≤200ms, Poor >500ms |
| `tbt_high` | Main thread blocked | M/B | 0.55-0.75 | Good ≤300ms, Poor >600ms |

**Common fixes:** Optimize images, defer JS, use CDN, code-split bundles.
