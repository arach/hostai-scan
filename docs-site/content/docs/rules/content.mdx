# Content Rules

Content rules account for 15% of the overall score. They evaluate whether the site's photos, descriptions, and messaging effectively sell the property.

## Why Content Matters

STR guests can't physically inspect a property before booking. They rely entirely on visual and written content to make decisions.

## Rules

### low_image_count

**Property has fewer than 10 images**

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Severity | Major |
| Impact | 0.60 |
| Effort | Medium |

Guests expect comprehensive photo coverage.

**Fix:** Add photos covering all rooms, outdoor spaces, and amenities.

---

### small_hero_image

**Hero image resolution too low**

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Severity | Minor |
| Impact | 0.35 |
| Effort | Low |

The hero image is the first visual impression. Low-resolution images look unprofessional.

**Threshold:** Hero image should be at least 1200px wide.

**Fix:** Replace with high-resolution photography.

---

### no_video

**No video content for property**

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Severity | Trivial |
| Impact | 0.20 |
| Effort | Medium |

Video walkthroughs are increasingly expected for premium properties.

**Fix:** Add a property walkthrough video.

---

### thin_description

**Property description too short**

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Severity | Minor |
| Impact | 0.40 |
| Effort | Low |

Brief descriptions fail to communicate what makes the property special.

**Threshold:** Description should be at least 100 words.

**Fix:** Expand to cover unique features, layout, amenities, and neighborhood.

---

### no_direct_booking_benefits

**No "why book direct" messaging**

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Severity | Minor |
| Impact | 0.45 |
| Effort | Low |

If guests find the same property on Airbnb, why should they book directly?

**Fix:** Add messaging about direct booking benefits: better rates, no service fees.

---
